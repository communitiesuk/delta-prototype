Making Delta simpler and faster for you

We are dedicated to improving your experience with Delta, making it easier and more efficient for you. To achieve this, we are updating the names and locations of various elements you interact with.

New labels and relationships

  • Collection Group: Provides information on the collection, its purpose, and how the data is used. The new collection group page lists all collections, both past and present.
    Example: Hedgehog Census
  • Collection: A one-off request for a return.
    Example: Hedgehog Census 2023
  • Return: The data submitted for a collection. A return may consist of one or more records, depending on the collection type.
    Example: Waverley Hedgehog Census 2023
  • Record: A set of data forming part of a return.
    Example: Waverley Hedgehog Census 2023
