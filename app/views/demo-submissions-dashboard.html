Why are we redesigning the submission dashboard?

  • Make collection manager's lives easier (simpler and faster)
  • Better support, more accurate data
  • Not getting rid of anything: cosmetic and functional changes only
  • Aligning with new Delta 2.0 look and feel (UI)
  • Laying foundations for accessibility and analytics improvements

Scope and phased delivery

  • Not a full redesign
  • Evolution not revolution
  • First phase of several: replicate existing capabilities and implement quick wins
