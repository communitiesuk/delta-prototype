Wayfinding goals

Why are we doing this?

'The platform is not easy to navigate, as I use it infrequently to certify claims I can forget where the claims are on the platform. It would help if there was a clear link on the home page to 'certify claims'.

  • Better UX for external users (Data Providers and Data Certifiers).
  • Better UX for internal users coming next.
  • Faster; one-click access to the things they need to work on (open collections)
  • Error reduction: easier routes to checking data (omissions and accuracy)
  • Reduction in missed deadlines
  • Remove need to use Collections Dashboard and the Data Store (and associated confusion)
  • Implement consistent user-centred naming conventions (Collections > Submission > Record(s))

Why are these new designs awesome?

  • 99% of the time users will have one-click access the thing they need to access
  • Friendlier
  • Clearer
  • Simpler
  • Faster (did we mention inline data previews?!
  • Cross promote account area and impact of Delta (better navigation)
  • Creating foundation for 'deep linking' (e.g. email linking straight to record requiring certification)
