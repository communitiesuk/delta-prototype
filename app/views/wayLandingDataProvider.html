In the mind of Data Providers and Data Certifiers...

  • A Collection is a request for data to be submitted by the Data Provider through the Delta platform
  • A Collection has a window during which data can be submitted (a 'Collection Window')
  • A Submission is the data provided for a collection
  • A Submission consists of one or more Records
  • The term 'Instance' or 'Collection Instance' means nothing for Data Providers and should not be used in external documents or discussions
