January 2024

Executive summary

December and January are the first full months of data and start to give us a baseline. Key insights for me so far include the high percent of data we receive vial a handful of XML uploads. And the relatively high rate of support required compared to the amount of open collections and error rates on the service. These suggest to me we have room to make the service much better for folks.

— Laurence Mallows
