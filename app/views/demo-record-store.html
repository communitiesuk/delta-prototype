Setting the scene

  • Minor cosmetic changes
  • No loss of functionality
  • Hierarchy
    • Collection group > Collection > Return > Record
  • A new name for the Data Store: Record Store
  • Design: static wireframe with dummy (hedgehog) data

Why are we redesigning the record store?

  • Aligning with new Delta 2.0 look and feel (UI)
  • Accessibility improvements
  • Improved ways of collaboration (link sharing)
  • Reduction in use by external users (data providers) due to improvements to collections entity page (related collections)
  • Expected updates to how internal users, such as collection managers, will access the data store and use filters.
  • Data protection
