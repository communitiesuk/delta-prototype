Reference designs for notification banners and panels

Completion vs creation

https://dluhcdigital.atlassian.net/browse/DT-858

  • Panels are displayed on completion of a process. This is typically at the end of creating something, like a dataset. Panels are typically displayed on the 'entity' summary page (acting in part like a header). Often summary content and next actions are listed beneath.
  • Notifications banners used to confirm the completion of an action - like uploading a document or associating an organisation. Unlike panels banners contain content that relates to previous screen (the last thing the user was doing).

  • GDS notification banner
