Overview for external users

Delta improvements

We are using your feedback to improve Delta. These improvements will make it easier and faster to use. We will tell you what we will change and when. The first changes will take place on [date]. They are:

New homepage

Your new homepage will show you:

  • open and missed collections
  • records that need certifying
  • what has been submitted
  • deadlines you need to meet

You will still be able to access the same links as before. Access XX by selecting the link at the top of the page.

Collection pages

There will be a page for each collection. This will make it easier to:

  • view related collections
  • view previous submissions
  • see who has worked on the return
  • see all relevant returns and records
  • find out how to get support
  • find out why we ask you for this data and what we use it for

We are not changing the permissions you need to view or certify data.

Record pages

This is a new page to vie a summary of a record. You can see this page by selecting a record on the collections page.

It will show:

  • the name of the record
  • the organisation
  • the collection
  • the status
  • who last updated/submitted/certified and when

How to use Delta

See our help and guidance pages for more information on how to use Delta.

Overview for internal users

Delta improvements

We are using your feedback to improve Delta.

These improvements will take place over the next few months, and will:

  • improve data quality
  • reduce support requests
  • make it easier to access support
  • improve the audit trail

We will tell you what we will change and when.

The first changes will take place on [date]. They are:

New homepage

Data providers will have a new homepage. It will show them:

  • open and missed collections
  • records that need certifying
  • what has been submitted
  • deadlines they need to meet

Collection pages

There will be a page for each collection. This will make it easier for data providers to:

  • view related collections
  • view previous submissions
  • see who has worked on the return
  • see all relevant returns and records
  • find out how to get support
  • find out why we ask them for this data and what we use it for

The permissions needed to view or certify data will not change.

Record pages

This is a new page to view a summary of a record. Data providers can see this page by selecting a record on the collections page.

It will show:

  • the name of the record
  • the organisation
  • the collection

  • the status
  • who last updated, submitted or certified it and when
