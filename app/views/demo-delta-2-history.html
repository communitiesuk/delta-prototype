Delta overview

Delta is a platform developed by MHCLG to handle large-scale data collection, primarily from local authorities, for policy development. It also plays a critical role in administering grants and supporting funding initiatives. As a crucial component of the UK's critical infrastructure, the platform manages a substantial volume of transactions every month and caters to a wide range of diverse use cases.

Key capabilities

  • CRM (orgs, individuals, roles)
  • Publishing platform
  • Asset management (documents)
  • Data capture (form, Excel upload, API/XML)
  • Data hand-off (CDS)
  • Reporting
  • Payments (in and out, SAP integration)

Typical monthly figures (2024)

  • 5,500 users (96% external, primarily Local Authorities)
  • 55 collections
  • 250,000 records
  • £5,000,000,000 in payments
