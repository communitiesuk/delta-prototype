Wayfinding goals - Data provider

Why are we doing this?

  • Better UX for Data Providers
  • Faster; on-click access to the things they need to work on (open collections)
  • Error reduction: easier routes to checking data (omissions and accuracy)
  • Reduced missed deadline
  • Remove need (and confusion) to use Collections Dashboard and the Data Store
  • Implement consistent user-centred naming conventions (Collections > Submission > Record(s))

Why are these new designs awesome?

  • Friendlier
  • Clearer
  • Simpler
  • Faster
  • Cross promote account area and impact of Delta
